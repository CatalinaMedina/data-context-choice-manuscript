## Teen pregnancy

I was inspired to do an updated version of the teen pregnancy data set in 
Chance, B. L., & Rossman, A. J. (2006). 
*Investigating statistical concepts, applications and methods*. 
Harrisonburg: Duxbury. 
<https://www.rossmanchance.com/iscam3/>.

To get more recent data I downloaded data from the CDC on [`teen pregnancy rates`](https://www.cdc.gov/nchs/pressroom/sosmap/teen-births/teenbirths.htm) (number of births per 1,000 females aged 15–19) and on `percent poverty` (percent of people in poverty in the past 12 months) by US state in 2022 reported in the [American Community Survey](https://www.census.gov/programs-surveys/acs/data.html). 
Look at `stats67-wi25-hw08-calcs.qmd` for how I cleaned and combined this data.
